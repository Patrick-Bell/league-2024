Football League

GOALS

If Goals are the same, whoever has a better ratio is ranked higher.

ASSISTS

If Assists are the same, whoever has a better ratio is ranked higher.

CLEAN SHEETS

If Clean Sheets are the same, whoever has a better ratio is ranked higher.

POINTS

If Points are the same, whoever has a better ratio is ranked higher.

GOAL CONTRIBUTOR

Calculated by Goals + Assists. If Contributions are the same, whoever has a better ratio is ranked higher.

UNBEATEN PERCENTAGE

Must play at least 5 games to qualify. If Unbeaten % is the same, whoever has played more games is ranked higher.

APPS

Each match is 45 minutes long. For this stat, extra time is not taken into consideration.

CARDS

Calculated by Yellow Cards + Red Cards. If Cards are the same, whoever has a higher ratio is ranked higher.
